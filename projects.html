Projects and Research

AI Drone for Potato Blight Detection

Collaborated with a few classmates to develop a drone-based system capable of detecting early stages of potato blight using image-based AI classification. Integrated trained models to identify symptoms across farmland plots and tested detection accuracy under variable light and soil conditions.

Myelin Foundry — Deep Learning for Satellite Imagery

Worked on visualising and classifying satellite images through deep-learning architectures including YOLO, UNet, and ResNet. Focused on cloud removal, segmentation, and clustering techniques to analyse terrain. Learned practical data cleaning, training, and experimentation workflows.

National Centre for Biological Sciences (NCBS) — Regeneration in Planarian Flatworms

Studied cellular regeneration by slicing planarian flatworms along different axes and examining their recovery patterns. Researched how positional cues and signal gradients drive tissue reconstruction. The work led to a publication analysing regeneration mechanisms.

Internship at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc)

Gained hands-on exposure to molecular biology, cell culture, and bioimaging processes at IISc, along with practical observation experiences.

Community Projects

Founded Weekend Warriors, a community effort supporting local families during the pandemic. Conducted volunteer tutoring and STEM workshops in public schools. Helped one student secure a national scholarship through personalised academic support.
